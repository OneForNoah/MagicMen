What is Magic: the Gathering?

Magic: the Gathering is a fantasy-based Trading Card Game developed and produced by Wizards of the Coast.

There are over 16,000 cards released so far, and more sets coming every few months.

There are 5 different colors -- White, Blue, Black, Red, and Green.

Each color has a spread of different attributes and abilities that cards in that color commonly have.

Games themselves are usually played between two people (duels) but some people or "formats" play as groups.

Each player starts at a preset lifetotal and the goal is to "defeat" the other player by dealing 20 damage to them.

Players play "Lands" which produce "mana", which is used to cast spells like "Creatures" and "Sorceries".
